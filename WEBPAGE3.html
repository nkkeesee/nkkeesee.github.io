Hobbies 
 
I have always been fascinated by computers but I was never really sure how to get into doing stuff with them. When I was little we didnt have a whole lot of money but we did have a desktop that my dad bought and any chance i could get i was on there learning how to use it. Then when I was in elementary school, we had a computer class and it was my favorite elective. It wasnt until I was out of highschool for almost three years, that i decided i wanted to go to school for cybersecurity. It all started when I got a 3d printer for christmas. I was big into modifying it and had to learn how to flash firmware. After learning that i became obsessed with computers and how they work and decided i wanted to go into the ethical hacking side of things. So thanks to 3d printing and coding as my hobbies, I am now learning to do what I am passionate about.
